Citation Policy
---------------

The continued development and maintenance of SAETASS depend on our ability to demonstrate its impact on the scientific field. If you use SAETASS to generate results, figures or data presented in a peer-reviewed publication, conference proceeding or thesis, **please, we kindly ask you to cite the project.**

Collaboration and Customization
-------------------------------

SAETASS is designed to be extensible. We actively encourage researchers to adapt the code for specific physical scenarios, such as exotic diffusion coefficients or unique wind profiles.

Support and co-authorship
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

We are open to formal collaborations to assist in the modification and customization of the source code. Our team can provide technical support regarding:

* Implementation of new boundary conditions.
* Optimization of numerical schemes for specific energy ranges.
* Integration of SAETASS into larger multi-messenger pipelines.

In cases where our team provides **significant technical or scientific contributions** to a project, it is standard academic practice to invite the contributing members of the SAETASS team to co-author the resulting publication.

If your research requires features beyond the current scope of SAETASS, or if you wish to discuss a collaborative project, please feel free to reach us out. You may contact us via GitHub Issues for feature requests or bug reports or contact the primary developer directly for private collaboration inquiries.
